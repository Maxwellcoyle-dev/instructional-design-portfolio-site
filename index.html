Data Security 101

Data Security 101 consists of 5 simple questions designed to help you think about your personal data security habits.
The course makes it easy to spot areas in your data security that could be improved and then gives you tips on where to start. After completing this quiz you'll receive a personalized Data Security Report.

There are 2 key design cencepts that I incorporated into this course, simplicity and gamification.
The experience aims to meet learners where they are by offering practical tips on how to improve online safety and presenting real data on stregnth and weknesses compared to others learners.

Third Party Vender Risk Management

Description
This project was developed for the Third Party Risk Management team of a fortune 500 corporation. The client wanted a course that would simplify the vender onboarding process and give the IT team clear direction regarding how to run new venders through the established risk assessment process.

Design
I worked directly with the client to identify the needs, rewrite the content they already had to be more focused on the project needs, and develop a fresh design concept. Once the new content and design concept was approved, I used Microsoft PowerPoint, Adobe Illustrator, and Articulate Storyline to develop the learning experience.

Tech Stack
Articulate Storyline, Adobe Illustrator, Microsoft PowerPoint, Snaggit

4/5

Treating Acute Stroke - Sales Training

Description
This course was developed as sales training for a medical device sales company. The company had a new product and the sales training manager needed to quickly train sales personnel to understand Stroke Treatment to advise surgeons in the operating room on how to best use the new device.

Design
In this situation, the sales trainer had already written down all of the information that the sales team needed to know. My first task was to restructure the content in a logical way and then determine the best way to make the content accessible to the learners. Finally, I developed the course in Articulate Storyline and Microsoft PowerPoint.

Tech Stack
Articulate Storyline, Microsoft PowerPoint

Host eLearning Content with AWS

Description
I developed this course to help other Instructional Designers host their learning content outside of an LMS. This course walks the learner through the entire process of publishing a storyline course and hosting it online using Amazon Web Services.
