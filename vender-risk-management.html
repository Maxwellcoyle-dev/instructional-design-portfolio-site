Third Party Risk Management

Overview

This project was developed for the IT Vendor Risk Management team of a fortune 500 corporation. The client wanted a course that would simplify the vender onboarding process and give the IT team clear direction regarding how to run new venders through the established risk assessment process.

Design Insights

Who were the learners?

IT personnel within a large pharmaceutical company who were asked to implement a new process for onboarding third-party venders.

What were the learning objectives?

After completing this training, learners will be able to:

  1. Identify their responsibilities when onboarding third party venders
  2. Serve as the point of contact for new venders
  3. Complete the necessary documentation & obtain approval for new venders using the Vender Risk Management Process
