Third Party Risk Management

Overview

This project was developed for the IT Vendor Risk Management team of a fortune 500 corporation. The client wanted a course that would simplify the vender onboarding process and give the IT team clear direction regarding how to run new venders through the established risk assessment process.

Design

I worked directly with the client to identify the needs, rewrite the content they already had to be more focused on the project needs, and develop a fresh design concept. Once the new content and design concept was approved, I used Microsoft PowerPoint, Adobe Illustrator, and Articulate Storyline to develop the learning experience.
