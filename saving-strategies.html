Saving Strategies

Overview

This course was developed for a national bank that wanted a tool for their salespeople to use with new customers. The bank wanted customers to see the power of saving small amounts of money over the long term.

Design

Initially, I designed this learning experience using Abode XD. Once the design concept was agreed upon, a fully functional version was quickly developed using Articulate Storyline.
