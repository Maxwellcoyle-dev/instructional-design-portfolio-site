Acute Stroke Basics

Overview

This course was developed as sales training for a medical device sales company. The client had a new product and the sales training manager needed to quickly train sales personnel to understand Stroke Treatment to advise surgeons in the operating room on how to best use the new device.

Design Insights

Who were the learners?

The audience for this training were sales reps within a neurovascular medical device company.

What were the learning objectives?

To be successful selling new stroke treatment devices, sales reps needed to have a solid foundation in anatomy, heart disease, diagnosing & imaging, and Treatment & management. This course dives into the realm of stroke treatment and management.

After completing this training, learners will be able to:

  1. Explain the procedure and products used to gain access and treat neurovascular disease.
  2. Demonstrate understanding of how the companyâ€™s products fit into various treatment pathways.
  3. Explain treatments and results for treating stroke & aneurysm (medical & Interventional)

How were the learning objectives met?

The course first teaches the content through short readings, videos, and interactions. Next, it challenges learners to apply what they are learning with non-graded scenario questions. Finally, it assesses their knowledge of the material understanding of the learning objectives.
