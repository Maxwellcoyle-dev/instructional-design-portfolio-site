This Learning Experience
Runs on xAPI.

Put simply, xAPI makes it possible to collect data for 100% of learning experiences.


In the past, using SCORM, L&D departments were only able to record very basic data from eLearning courses (completion, final exam score). On the other hand, xAPI data collection is not limited to an eLearning and allows us to record any data point imaginable.

xAPI has the power to collect in-depth data from eLearning courses, slack communications, social media posts, and customer interactions in sales force. All of this data can be compiled and used to fine tune learning experiences to continually improve KPI’s.

Why Data Security?

Data Security has become a major focus in 2022.

A quick Google search can shed light on how many large companies have suffered from data security issues in the last few years. Large sums of money have been spent on data security training and monitoring geared to prevent these mishaps, yet they continue year in and year out.

At the core of the issue are humans who have deeply rooted habits and are likely to take the path of least resistance. This learning experience seeks to make data security upskilling accessable and practical for anyone. Data Security 101 does this by focusing on the individual and how they stand to benefit from better data security skills. Learners can see how their skills and behaviors stack up against other individuals and get practical tips and resources that they can implement right away.
